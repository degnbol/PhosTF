From publication "Comparison of transcription regulatory interactions inferred from high-throughput methods: what do they reveal?" by Balaji et al. 2008
The edges are collected from a P-value threshold of ChIP-chip data. Threshold<=.001. Sources: Lee 2002, Harbinson 2004, Horak 2002 according to suppl. pdf at https://ars.els-cdn.com/content/image/1-s2.0-S0168952508001558-mmc1.pdf
TRNCC are chip chip.
TRNGRD are gene deletion with data from Hu 2007.
TRNGROE are overexpression from Chua 2006.
